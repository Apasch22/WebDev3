Welcome to the guide of basic positions of American Football, down below are 3 key positions for both offensse and defense, Please note that these are KEY positions and not all of the positions in American Football.

Offense

Quarter Back-Leader of the time, main job is to throw the football and hand the ball to running back when needed to.

Running Back-The Bus of the team, main job is to run the ball and can sometimes be a blocker for the QB.

Wide Receiver-The Playmaker of the team, main job is to run the route they are given and catch the ball when targeted, sometimes they may be a blocker for run plays going into their direction.

Defense

Middle Linebacker-The heart of the defense, main job is to protect the middle of the field and stop any run plays up the middle, this position also has a lot of hard hitter on RB and WR.

Cornerback-The PickPocket of the defense, main job is to stop the WR from catching the football by a deflection or an interception. The Cornerback can argueably the hardest position in Football behind the O-line and QB.

Defensive Line-The Powerhouse of the defense, main job is to sack the QB at all costs or tackle the running at all costs to cause struggles for the opposing team.
