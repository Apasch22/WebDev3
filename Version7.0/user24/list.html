Here are some of the most hardest positions to play in the NFL

1) The Cornerback is arguably the hardest position to play in football because they must be able to react in a fast amount of time having to be in the game mentally and physically. Being a great cornerback requires good speed, good reaction time, fast agility and always staying mentally focused. It makes it a lot harder to guard Wide Receivers that have amazing route running and speed.
