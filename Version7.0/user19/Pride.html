P.R.I.D.E has many important acronims that sets young men up to become men in the real world and be leaders.

Passion

- Passion means you do what you love, and you want to improve everyday and become your better self.

Respect

- Respect is one of the most important out of all of them, you should have respect for your piers and teammates.

Integrity

- Integrity is what you do when no one is looking, are you being a good person or are you a bad person.

Discipline

- Discipline is important because you should always listen to your coaches and you should always give your best

Energy

You should always give positive energy to the team and give it your all.
