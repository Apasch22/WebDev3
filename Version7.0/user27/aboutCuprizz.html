About Cuprizz Coffee

Cuprizz Coffee was started by Mikhail Yasnuk's dad in Shakopee, MN.
Since 2015, a little roastery called Cuprizz has been fueling the Shakopee community with magical coffee. Too often, brands hide behind good marketing and branding without paying enough attention to the actual stuff they're selling — but not Cuprizz. The roaster is super transparent with where it sources its coffee, and it's for the best for both the coffee farmers and the consumers.
